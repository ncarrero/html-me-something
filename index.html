My Europe Trip



In the summer of 2015, my three friends and I went on a trip of a lifetime through the company EF College Break.  Over the course of 12 days, we saw and experienced the culture, food, and more of four different European countries.  Below you will see some of the places my friends and I visited while on our trip to Europe in July 2015.



Days 1-3: London, United Kingdom

For the first three days, we explored London, England


Day 1 Day 2 Day 3

Our trip began at the airport. We had an 8 hour nonstop flight from JFK to Heathrow.

We started our first day by landing and taking a car to our hotel.

We arrived at our hotel, settled in, and started introducing ourselves to the people we would be traveling with for the next 10 days.


Days 4-6: Paris, France

We took an underground / underwater train to Paris, France


Day 4 Day 5 Day 6

We were able to take a bus tour around the city.

Half of the group took a trip inside Versailles. My group chose to walk around and visit some sites we saw on the bus tour. I was especially happy to go to Louis Vuitton!

On our last day, we took a tour of the Louvre Museum. When we got out, we were able to see part of the Tour de France.



Days 7-9: Barcelona, Spain

We took a small plane over from Paris to Barcelona


Day 7 Day 8 Day 9

We took yet another bus tour of this beautiful city. We were able to stop at Park Guell to take in the beautiful artwork and architecture of some of Gaudi's buildings.

We took an amazing bicycle tour through the alleys and parks and ended up at the beach.

We ended our trip with an amazing dancing show and at a restaurant with an amazing view of the city.


Days 10-12: Coimbra, Portugal

We took another small plane from Paris to Porto, where my friend's cousin picked us up


Day 10 Day 11 Day 12

We arrived in Porto and went to my friend's cousin's house. We stayed there for the duration of the trip.

We took a trip to Coimbra, the closest city. We were able to walk around and see the culture and architecture.

On our last day, we took a two-hour bus ride to the Lisbon airport to head back home to America. Before we left, we said goodbye to her grandmother, who had beautiful artwork of her daughters on the outside wall of her house.
